The Problem

The envelope is the bottleneck

At most feiseanna, a judge writes scores on paper. The paper goes in an envelope. The envelope gets carried to a collection box. Someone opens it and manually types every score into a system. Then tabulation runs. Every step adds latency. Every hand-off adds error risk. Every minute a score sits in an envelope is a minute dancers, parents, and organizers are waiting.

How It Works

Four people, four screens, one feis

FeisTab matches how a real feis actually runs. Each role gets their own view with exactly what they need.

💻

Registration Desk

/registration

Dancer walks up, desk person searches by name, assigns the next number. One tap = checked in across all their competitions.

  • Search by name or school
  • Auto-incrementing numbers
  • Progress tracker
📋

Side-Stage

/checkin

The person at the stage confirms who's present for each competition before the judge starts. Marks no-shows and scratches.

  • Competitions grouped by urgency
  • Per-dancer status dropdowns
  • Roster confirmation gate
✍️

Judge

/judge

Sees their assigned competitions. Taps "Start" when ready. Enters scores on their phone. Signs off when done. The system handles the rest.

  • Assignment-based competition list
  • One-tap score entry per dancer
  • Sign-off locks scores
🎯

Organizer

/dashboard

Imports rosters, assigns judges, monitors progress. Once all judges sign off, previews results, approves, and publishes.

  • CSV roster import
  • Judge assignment
  • Tabulation & publish control

Why It's Different

Built for how feiseanna actually work

Not a generic scoring app. Built from conversations with real organizers about real problems.

📷

Numbers assigned at the door

Dancers get their competitor number when they check in on event day. Just like it's always been done, but now it's in the system instantly.

📄

Paper judges still welcome

Tabulator mode lets someone enter scores from paper sheets. Judges who aren't ready for phones don't have to change. The system adapts.

🔒

Judges control their own start

No waiting for the organizer to click buttons. The judge taps "Start" when they're ready. Roster confirmed = ready to go.

🔎

Problems caught before results

Missing scores, incomplete packets, suspicious patterns. Flagged automatically before tabulation runs. Not after results are posted.

📜

Full audit trail

Every score entry, sign-off, correction, and publish is logged. If a parent asks how their dancer placed 4th, you can show them exactly.

🔄

Safe corrections

Need to fix a score after sign-off? Unlock, document the reason, fix, re-tabulate. Every change on the record. Clean.

The score goes from the judge's phone directly into the system. No envelope, no box, no transcription. What used to take 30 minutes takes seconds.
